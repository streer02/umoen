UMOEN Founders

Since our inception, our founders have worked towards more uniform standards across the field of oncology. Please feel free to ask questions via email!

President

Jeremy Whalen, PharmD, BCOP Jeremy Whalen, PharmD, BCOP is the Oncology Pharmacy Supervisor for North Memorial Medical Center's Humphrey Cancer Center in Robbinsdale, Minnesota. He completed his Doctor of Pharmacy at Creighton University in Omaha, NE and then a PGY1 Residency at Maine Medical Center in Portland, Maine and was the first PGY2 Oncology Specialty Resident at the University of Minnesota Medical Center in Minneapolis, Minnesota. Jeremy received his Board Certification in Oncology Pharmacy (BCOP) in 2010. He has been active as the primary preceptor for both student and resident oncology rotations and currently accepts students from both Creighton University and University of Minnesota.

Vice President

Paul Forsberg, PharmD, BCOP Paul Forsberg, PharmD, BCOP is a Clinical Pharmacy Coordinator at St. John's Hospital, HealthEast Care System. Paul received his PharmD degree from the University of Montana. Paul is a member of the Minnesota Society of Health System Pharmacists (MSHP) and the Hematology/Oncology Pharmacy Association (HOPA). At HealthEast Paul serves on various committees including Oncology Shared Nursing Leadership Council, P&T Committee, Co-Chair of the P&T Hematology/Oncology Subcommittee, CCOP IRB, St. John's Residency Advisory Committee, and Med Safety Work Group. Paul received his Board Certification in Oncology Pharmacy (BCOP).

Secretary

Shirley Kern, RN, MSN, APRN-BC, AOCN Shirley Kern, RN, MSN, APRN-BC, AOCN is an Oncology Clinical Nurse Specialist at North Memorial Medical Center in Robbinsdale, Minnesota. She has been practicing as an oncology nurse for nearly twenty years. Shirley graduated from Creighton University in Omaha, Nebraska in 1992 with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Psychology and in 1994 with a Bachelor of Science degree in Nursing. She went on to complete her Master of Science degree in Nursing at the University of Iowa in 1998. Shirley works with a multidisciplinary medical team at North Memorial to coordinate care and navigate patients through the hospital and clinic systems. Shirley has been very involved in the community with oncology nursing and most recently served as the 2012 President of the Metro Minnesota Chapter of the Oncology Nursing Society. She is excited to continue the journey of advocating for cancer patients.

Treasurer

Monique Giordana, PharmD, BCOP Monique Giordana, PharmD, BCOP is an Oncology Clinical Pharmacy Specialist currently practicing in both inpatient and outpatient hematology/oncology care. Monique received her Doctor of Pharmacy from the University of Minnesota College of Pharmacy. She completed a general practice residency (PGY1) at the Dallas VA Medical Center in Dallas, Texas and a specialized hematology/oncology residency (PGY2) at Mayo Clinic in Rochester, Minnesota. Monique's interest in oncology arose during pharmacy school while working in a local oncology clinic. The impact pharmacists had on the patients' quality of life had a remarkable influence in her future training and career path. During residency, she developed a passion for research and started a career working within the pharmaceutical industry on oncology education and research learning many valuable skills including drug development before returning to patient care. Monique's passion for starting the Upper Midwest Oncology Education Network with colleagues stemmed from the need for current and exciting educational opportunities for oncology healthcare professionals.
